# Flashpoint Risk Index (FRI)

A public, read-only tool to check protest or civil-unrest risk levels by date and location.

## How to use
1. Open `index.html` in your browser.
2. Enter a date, state, and optional city/county.
3. Click **Check Risk** to view the risk level and safety advisory.

## Privacy
No tracking, no analytics, and no data stored.
